How TO Make Screencast Videos

Step 4 - Publish the video

Navigation: Home Step 1 Step 2 Step 3

Here are some ways of sharing a video with your students.


Upload it to YouTube

This is probably the easiest way to share videos, as long as everyone has a relible Internet connection. You can send them the URL directly, or embed the YouTube video on another platform. This is the objectively best way of sharing videos if you want it to reach viewers beyond your classroom.


Upload it to Dropbox

This is a reasonable way to share videos, again as long as everyone has a relible Internet connection. You can send them the URL directly, and they can watch the video direclty in thir browser without needing to download it.


Upload it to a Learning Management System

If your class actively uses a Learning Management System (e.g., D2L, Moodle, Edmodo, Google Classroom), this is a reasonable way to share videos. You can upload the file directy, or first upload it to YouTube and embed the YouTube video. They can watch the video directly in the browser.


Burn it onto DVDs

This is a solution for how to share videos with students who do not have a reliable Internet connection. They can watch the videos on a DVD played or on a computer with no Internet connection needed.
