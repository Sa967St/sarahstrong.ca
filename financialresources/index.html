Financial Resources

Thinking about going to university or college? Learn about the financial options available to you!


Scholarships, Bursaries, and Grants

Scholarships, bursaries and grants are money awarded based on high grades and/or financial need. All universities offer financial options such as these.

You can find a full list of scholarships, bursaries, and grants here!

Most universities have outstanding excellence scholarships that are each awarded to one or two new students every year. You need fill out an application to be considered. Some application deadlines are as early as January. Meeting the minimum requirements is not enough to be selected for these scholarships.

Loans

You can apply for loans to pay for the costs associated with post-secondary education. After you finish your studies, you can slowly pay back your loans at an affordable rate.

  • The most common way students in Ontatio get loans is through Ontario Student Assistance Program, abbreviated OSAP for short. Click here to learn more about OSAP.

  • Banks and credit unions offer loans called lines of credit/. Contact your financial institution to see if you are eligible for a line of credit
