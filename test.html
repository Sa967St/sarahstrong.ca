Mathematics


Gr. 9 Applied Math - MPM 1D

Clarkson Secondary School, Mississauga, ON

Topics: Rates, Polynomial Operations, Solving Equations

  • Posted and discussed learning goals daily so students knew what they were learning each class.
  • Used a variety of strategies to take up homework and determined which ones students found most effective.
  • Created group and class activities to promote student collaboration.
  • Created differentiated tasks using manipulatives such as Algebra Tiles which helped students grasp concepts in different ways.

Gr. 10 Applied Math - MPM 2D (2 sections)

Rick Hansen Secondary School, Mississauga, ON

Topics: Right Triangle Trigonometry, 3D Measurement, Linear Relations

  • Created hands-on activities using manipulatives such as Giant Geosolids and Folding Geometric Shapes.
  • Used Socrative and Nearpod to engage students and to collect data which enhanced future lessons.
  • Created differentiated assessments which allowed students to effectively express what they had learned.
  • Assisted students with studying and homework after school through the Counting On You program.

Gr. 12 Advanced Functions- MHF 4U

Clarkson Secondary School, Mississauga, ON

Topics: Polynomial Functions, Polynomial Equations and Inequalities

  • Created a unit test using LaTeX to familiarize students with a common layout of a university exam.

Gr. 12 Advanced Functions - MHF 4U

Rick Hansen Secondary School, Mississauga, ON
