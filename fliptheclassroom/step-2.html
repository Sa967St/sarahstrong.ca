Step 2 - Record the video

After you have all the software and hardware ready to go, here's how you would record the video. Skip the steps that don't apply to you.

  1. Open the drawing software.
  2. Set up the drawing tablet.
  3. Set up the webcam and turn it on.
  4. Start capturing the screen and audio.
  5. Deliver the lesson.
    • Use the pen and tablet to draw in the drawing software.
    • Speak into the microphone.
  6. Stop capturing the screen and audio.
  7. Turn off the webcam.
  8. Save the files.
