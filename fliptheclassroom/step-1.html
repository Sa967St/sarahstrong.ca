Step 1 - Acquire software and hardware

Drawing Software

You may need drawing software to write and draw on during your lesson.

You can use the Ink Annotation feature of PowerPoint to write and draw on the slides.
You do not need to have the desktop version of Office installed -- you can use the online version for free.
SmoothDraw is a free drawing software. It is the one used in Khan Academy videos videos.
You can download SmoothDraw here: http://www.smoothdraw.com/sd

Microphone

Any cheap USB microphone or headset you can find will probably suffice. You may want to avoid using your computer's microphone because it will pick up other sounds such as your computer's fan.

Screen Capture and Voice Recording Software

  • Camtasia Studio
