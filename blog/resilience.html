What Resilience Is

Resilience is the ability to rebuild and grow from adversity. It is a trait that ever human is capable of developing. For children, the key for developing it is having caring and supportive relationships with adults

Classroom Activities that Promote Resilience

  • Read and discuss relatable stories in which the characters overcome challenges.
